Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Royal Throne ("the App") is a mobile application designed to help you track your bathroom sessions. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

The App uses anonymous authentication by default. No email, name, or identifying info is required. You may optionally link an email for recovery, set a display name, or choose an avatar.

Session Data

When you log a session, we store: start/end timestamps, duration, your rating, and any notes you add.

Chat Messages

Messages in buddy chats and group rooms are stored with timestamps. Chat messages are automatically deleted after 30 days.

Technical Information

Minimal device info (OS version, app version, crash logs). We do NOT use third-party analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

What we DO

Provide the app features (session logs, chat, stats), fix bugs, send local on-device notifications (not from our servers).

What we NEVER do

  • Sell your data to third parties.
  • Share it with analytics providers.
  • Build marketing profiles.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted in the US East region. We use HTTPS/TLS encryption, row-level security policies, and anonymous auth by default.

4. Your Rights

View your data anytime in the app. Delete your account and ALL data permanently from Settings. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

5. Notifications

All notifications are generated on your device. No push tokens are sent to our servers. Disable anytime in your device settings.

6. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, contact us and we will delete their data.

7. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for backend infrastructure. We do not integrate any third-party social media trackers or analytics platforms.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at royalthroneapp@gmail.com
